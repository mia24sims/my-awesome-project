Meet the Belchers


Bob's Burgers is an American animated sitcom created by Loren Bouchard for the Fox Broadcasting Company. It is centered on the Belcher family
(parents Bob and Linda and their three children, Tina, Gene, and Louise) who run a burger restaurant and often go on adventures of many kinds.
